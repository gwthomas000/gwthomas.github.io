Why Prussia?

I have always been interested in everything history, especially 20th century Europe. Germany is rich in history and, while it can be confusing at times, is very interesting. I thought that discussing about the history of Prussia, the precursor to Germany, would make for a unique and interesting topic to make my website about.

My Growth

This was my very first web page I had made after about 1 month of learning html. While I was very proud of it then, I feel like I have grown a lot since. On my first page there was no css used since I did not know how to code css, making the site look very barren. Yet I still feel like I displayed my skills well, especially with the timeline table.
